<- Heavy Cruiser -----------------------------------------------------------
The Heavy Cruiser class is a child of the Cruiser class. Its constructing 
characteristics are as follows:

name: Big Boi
width: 2
height: 5
hull integrity: 6
shield: 1
speed: 12
maneuverability: 4

It uses the HeavyAutomatic weapon trait to fire.
